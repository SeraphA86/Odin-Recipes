Creamy Hot Cocoa

It's old fashioned, it's comforting, it makes the kitchen smell wonderful and it's good for the soul.

Ingredients

  1.   ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  2.   ¾ cup white sugar
  3.   1 pinch salt
  4.   ⅓ cup boiling water
  5.   3 ½ cups milk
  6.   ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  7.   ½ cup half-and-half cream

Directions

  Combine the cocoa, sugar and pinch of salt in a saucepan. Blend in the boiling water.
  Bring this mixture to an easy boil while you stir. Simmer and stir for about 2 minutes.
  Watch that it doesn’t scorch. Stir in 3 ½ cups of milk and heat until very hot, but do not boil!
  Remove from heat and add vanilla. Divide between 4 mugs.
  Add the cream to the mugs of cocoa to cool it to drinking temperature.
