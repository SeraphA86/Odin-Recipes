Snow Flake Cocoa

It's great for Christmas parties!

Ingredients

  1.   2 cups whipping cream
  2.   6 cups milk
  3.   1 teaspoon vanilla
  4.   1 (12 ounce) package white chocolate chips
  5.   Whipped cream
  6.   Candy canes

Directions

  Step 1

  Stir together the whipping cream, milk, vanilla, and white chocolate chips in a slow cooker.

  Step 2

  Cover and cook on low for 2 to 2 Â½ hours, stirring occasionally, until mixture is hot and chocolate chips are melted.
  Stir again before serving. Garnish with whipped cream and candy canes, as desired.
