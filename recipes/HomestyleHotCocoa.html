Homestyle Hot Cocoa

Perfect for snowy winter nights!

Ingredients

  1.   ½ cup white sugar
  2.   ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  3.   ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  4.   1 pinch salt
  5.   ⅓ cup boiling water
  6.   1 cup half-and-half, divided
  7.   ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  8.   8 large marshmallows (Optional)

Directions

  Step 1

  Whisk together sugar, cocoa powder, cinnamon, and salt in a saucepan.
  Stir in boiling water. Whisk until sugar is dissolved.

  Step 2

  Bring cocoa mixture to a simmer over medium-high heat, stirring constantly, about 2 minutes.
  Stir milk and ½ cup half-and-half into water mixture.
  Cook and stir just until hot, about 2 minutes.
  Remove saucepan from heat.
  Stir in remaining half-and-half and vanilla extract.

  Step 3

  Divide cocoa into mugs, top with marshmallows, and serve.
